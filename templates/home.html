About Aafrin Creations

Traditional Punjabi attire for women typically includes the vibrant and graceful salwar kameez, adorned with intricate embroidery or vibrant prints, paired with a colorful dupatta. Men often wear the distinctive Punjabi kurta, complemented by loose-fitting pants known as pajama or dhoti, and topped with a colorful turban, reflecting their cultural and regional identity. The attire is not just clothing but a symbol of Punjabi pride and exuberance, showcasing a blend of tradition and modernity that resonates across the globe.
